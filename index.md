# Welcome
This website is a collection of notes, tips and tricks that I've collected over the years. It mainly focuses on Linux, software, coding, programming, cyber security and other computer related topics. It is a second brain, if you will. Some pages are nicely set up, others may be a bit rougher as they are scribbled down quickly. I may, or I may not style these pages at a later date. We'll see.

Feel free to use the content of this site for your own purposes. If you have any suggestions, questions or other feedback, please let me know.
